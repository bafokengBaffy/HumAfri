The Lesotho Hoodie is part of our African Unity Collection, featuring authentic Basotho blanket patterns. Each hoodie is:

  • Made from 80% cotton and 20% polyester for comfort and durability
  • Features a kangaroo pocket and adjustable drawstring hood
  • Screen-printed with traditional Lesotho motifs that won't fade
  • Machine washable with colors that stay vibrant
  • Designed and crafted in Lesotho by local artisans
  • Available in multiple sizes for men and women
